ARTIFACTS

What artifacts are there?

There is a bunch of different artifacts that you have to carefully distrubute between your characters so that they can reach their highest potential. Question is, what artifacts are there, and for what characters are what artifacts good?

What do i need them for?

Artifacts are used to empower your characters, thanks so them, you can hit BIG NUMBERS. Each character can be built X different ways, so it is up to you to choose which ones will suit your idea better.

Where can i farm them?

They can be farmed in domains that you can unlock all over Teyvat. Some domains require more brain work for their unlock and some require zero. There are a few artifacts that cannot be farmed via normal means. Getting those is significantly more difficult, since you can only obtain them through weekly bosses, ordinary bosses and quests.
